About Our College

Unique College was founded in 1965 with a vision to provide quality education. Our institution has grown from a small community college to a top-ranked educational hub.

Our mission is to foster an environment of academic excellence and provide students with the skills they need to excel in their careers and contribute to society.

We value diversity, inclusivity, innovation, and integrity, striving to maintain the highest standards of education and community service.

Our Courses

Explore a variety of courses designed to help you achieve your educational and career goals.

Bachelor of Science in Computer Science

An undergraduate program designed to provide a strong foundation in computer science principles, including programming, algorithms, and software development.

  • Duration: 3-4 years
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics
  • Course Fees: ₹ 1,50,000/year
Master of Business Administration (MBA)

A postgraduate program focusing on business management principles, including marketing, finance, human resources, and strategic management.

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in any discipline
  • Course Fees: ₹ 3,00,000/year
Diploma in Graphic Design

A short-term program aimed at providing practical skills in graphic design, including the use of design software, color theory, and visual communication.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Eligibility: 10+2 or equivalent
  • Course Fees: ₹ 50,000
